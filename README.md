# Pizza-Sales-Power-BI-Dashboard
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of pizza sales performance across various metrics including revenue, quantity, size, category and orders.
Key Insights:

1. Top-Level Performance (KPIs)
- Overall revenue: $817,860.05
- Total orders placed: 21,350
- Average Order Value (AOV): $38.31
- Total pizzas sold: 49,574

2. Sales & Order Trends (Time-Based)
- Busiest Days: Friday and Saturday are peak days for both orders and revenue.
- Peak Time: The highest number of orders occurs during the 12:00 PM (Lunch) hour, followed by the 5:00 PM hour (early dinner/after work), indicating strong demand for quick lunch options and evening meals.
- Monthly Seasonality:
 - Peak Months: January and July show the highest total number of orders, suggesting potential promotional or holiday spikes.
 - Low Month: October shows the lowest order volume, which could be a focus area for targeted marketing.

3. Product Performance (What Sells)
- Top Pizza Category: The Classic category is the most popular, dominating both total orders and revenue share.
- Preferred Size: Large is the most preferred pizza size by a significant margin for both total orders and total sales.
- Top Sellers (Revenue): The Thai Chicken Pizza is the number one revenue generator.
- Top Sellers (Quantity/Orders): The Classic Deluxe Pizza leads in terms of the sheer number of units sold and total orders placed.
- Worst Seller (Actionable Insight): The Brie Carre Pizza is the lowest performer across all key metrics (Revenue, Quantity, and Orders).
